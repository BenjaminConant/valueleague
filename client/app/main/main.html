Value League

One day.

One stock.

One dollar.

1. Join a game.

Each day is a new game. Pick a date anywhere from tomorrow or in the future to join that game.

2. Pick a stock.

Choose from any publicly traded stock. Pick one that you think will increase the most in a day.

3. Put a dollar in the pool.

It's only a dollar to play. Play as many as you want. We're using Zipmark for easy and secure payment.

4. See price changes over a day.

See how your stock pick changes over the day real-time.

5. See how your stock ranks.

Compare how your pick ranks against others in the game.

6. Collect winnings.

At the end of the day, those whose stock gained the most that day wins the pool.
